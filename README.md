# Excel-Sales-Analytics

## Context:
Atliq Hardware specializes in selling a wide range of hardware products such as PCs, printers, and various peripheral devices. Apart from selling this wide lineup of products, it also manufactures it's own products. To reach it's customers, it utilizes intermediaries such as retail big names like Best Buy and Costco, popular online marketplaces like Amazon, as well as it's own Brick and Mortar stores. In order to get an overview of their performance with respect to Revenue and Finance, AtliQ needs some comprhensive data backed reports, that will also help them to make business decisions.

## Sales Report :

- **Project objective:** 

    **1.** To create a _[customer performance report](https://github.com/AbhiPundir98/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)_ 

    **2.** Conduct a comprehensive comparison between _[market performance and sales targets]( https://github.com/AbhiPundir98/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Market%20Performance%20vs%20Target.pdf )_

- **Purpose of sales analytics reports:** Empower businesses to monitor and evaluate their sales activities and performance.

- **Importance of analyzing sales data:** Identify sales patterns and track key performance indicators (KPIs).

- **Role of reports:** Determine effective customer discounts, facilitate negotiations with consumers, and identify potential business expansion opportunities in promising countries.


## Finance Report :

- **Project objective:** 

    **1.** To create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by _[Fiscal Year](https://github.com/AbhiPundir98/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20by%20Fiscal%20Years.pdf)_ 

   **2.** Create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by _[Markets](https://github.com/AbhiPundir98/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20by%20Markets%20FY%202021.pdf)_

   **3.** Create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by _[Months & Quarters](https://github.com/AbhiPundir98/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20by%20FY%20Months%20%26%20Quarters.pdf)_

- **Purpose of sales analytics reports:** Evaluation of financial performance, support decision-making, and facilitate communication with stakeholders.

- **Importance of analyzing Finance data:** Aid in benchmarking against industry peers and previous periods Foundation for budgeting and forecasting.

- **Role of reports:** Align financial planning with strategic goals Instill confidence in the organization's financial outlook.
